Elevator Styles

ELE Imperforated ELE Swing Door ELE Telescopic Door ELE Collapsible Door

We offer a wide and comprehensive range of Manual Door Elevators to Esteemed customers as per their requirements. Manual Doors are Handled Continuously for entering and exiting the elevator, hence these doors have to be very robust to take the continuous ware and tare. All our manual doors are equipped with mechanical interlocks and electrical safety system; to make the use of the elevator safe for the passengers.

We provide various types of manual doors are available to suit the site conditions and also to suit the needs of various customers.

Our Manual Door Elevator offer:

  • Solid Built & light weight.
  • Anticorrosive coating for enhanced life.
  • Concealed & Vandal proof door closer, handle & Hinges.
  • Concealed handle to enhance the aesthetics.
  • Opening width starting from 600 Mm to 800 MM.
  • Effective skin protection during installation to endure hostile site conditions.

Imperforated

We provide wide range of imperforated doors to our customers. Extensively used in apartments, commercial complex because of its safety and durability. These imperforate doors are made like the manual collapsible doors but have flaps attached to it. These flaps make sure that when the door is closed no opening is left in the doors. Cut outs are made in the door for the purpose of having a look on the other side of the door.These doors may be used for landings as well for car. Imperforate doors are available in Mild steel and Stainless steel. Mild steel doors are painted for corrosion resistance. These doors may be used instead of collapsible doors for extra safety.

Our Imperforated Door offer:

  • Self Door closing mechanism with adjustable speed
  • High durability
  • High strength
  • Longer service life
  • Smooth functioning

Swing Door

We bring forth a wide range of swing doors for our valuable customers. These doors help totally enclose the shaft. The swing doors have a vision panel having glass and steel mesh. A door closure is fitted to the swing door to make sure the closing is done perfectly. Swing doors available with Wooden Swing Doors, Mild steel swing doors and Glass Swing doors. Wooden swing doors are flush doors fitted with laminates. Quality swing doors are used to give long life and durability. Mild Steel Powder coated swing doors are supplied along with frames. These swing doors are durable and even with its Mild steel structure can be opened and closed with ease. Glass Swing Doors are Made of Toughened glass with Stainless steel border. These Swing Doors add to the aesthetic feel of the elevator.

Our Swing Door offer:

  • Plug and Play installation
  • Solid built with light weight
  • Maintenance free operation
  • Lifelong Dimensional Stability
  • Noiseless and smooth operation
  • Anti Corrosive coating for enhanced life
  • Concealed and vandle proof SS handle
  • Flushed glass vision with safety grill
  • Compatible for any kind of Door lock

Telescopic Door

We provide wide range of telescopic doors for our esteemed customers according to the need and industrial demands. Manual Telescopic Doors are made to slide in a telescopic fashion. Telescopic doors have Cut outs made in the door for the purpose of having a look on the other side of the door, these cut out are covered with glass and steel mesh. These doors are available in Mild steel, Stainless steel and Glass. Mild steel doors are painted for corrosion resistance.

Our Telescopic Door offer:

  • Least Maintenance
  • Self Door closing mechanism with adjustable speed
  • Anti Corrosive coating for enhanced life
  • Noiseless and smooth operation
  • Concealed and vandle proof handle
  • Dual Safety for door panel guide
  • Compatible for any kind of Door lock

Collapsible Door

Collapsible Doors are widely used in elevators placed in various sectors such as industrial, commercial and residential, these doors are widely appreciated by clients for their longer service life, corrosion resistance and fine finish. These doors are robust and since long have proved to be a simple yet effective solution for elevator doors hence, due to its reliability it is used for both Passenger as well as goods elevators.

These doors may be used for landings as well for car. These doors can be made to fit various sizes of openings, making it ideal solution for odd doors sizes.

Collapsible Doors are Available in mild steel colored black and also in stainless steel

Our Collapsible Door offer:

  • High security locking
  • Very low maintenance cost
  • Superior quality
  • Safety Sensor
  • Power Operated
